Job Summary

The COO role is a key member of the senior management team, reporting only to the Chief Executive Officer (CEO). He/she has to maintain control of diverse business operations, and he/she has to be an experienced and efficient leader.

Responsibilities and Duties
  • Establishes, implements, and communicates the strategic direction of the organization’s operational goals, collaborating with other divisions and departments to carry out the organization’s goals and objectives.
  • Oversee daily operations of the company and the work of executives (Sales, Marketing, and UW).
  • Provides leadership to ensure all activities are consistent with our goals, guidelines, and culture by monitoring trends and evolving our strategy to keep up with changing customer
  • Ensures that departmental decisions, project plans, and budgets such as those for staffing, development, and team execution align across the larger team.
Qualifications and Skills
  • Proven experience in Chief Operating Office or relevant role
  • Oversee daily operations of the company and the work of executives (Sales, Marketing, and UW).
  • Demonstrable competency in strategic planning and business development.
  • Working knowledge of data analysis and performance/operation metrics.
  • Working knowledge of IT/Business infrastructure and MS Office
